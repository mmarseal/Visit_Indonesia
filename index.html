MOUNT
KELIMUTU

Mount Kelimutu is a voicano located on Flores Island, NTT Province, Indonesia. The location of this mountain is precisely in Pemo Village

LABUAN
BAJO

Labuan Bajo is one of the urban villages in Komodo Sub-district, West Manggarai Regency, East Nusa Tenggara Province, Indonesia. Labuan Bajo is also the administrative center of Komodo Subdistrict and the capital of West Manggarai Regency.

RAJA AMPAT
ISLAND

The Raja Ampat Islands are an archipelago located in the western part of the Bird's Head Peninsula of Papua Island. Administratively, it falls under Raja Ampat Regency and Sorong City, Southwest Papua Province.

MOUNT
BROMO

Mount Bromo or in the Tengger language spelled "Brama", also called Tengger Caldera, is an active volcano in East Java, Indonesia.
